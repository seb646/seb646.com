Video Game Design Club

The Video Game Design Club was founded in 2017 by Co-Presidents Maxwell Kelly and Sebastian Rodriguez, with faculty advisor Saber Khan. The club was centered around the idea of educating Browning students on Video Game Design. The club's leadership later expanded to include retired Club Penguin gold member Alfonso Laffont.
